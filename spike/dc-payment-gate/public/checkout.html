The localhost helper signs an OpenID4VP request (self-signed reader cert) and hands it here. This page calls navigator.credentials.get({digital}) for an EMVCo Digital Payment Credential (urn:emvco:dpc:card:1, or the Multipaz mdoc form). Desktop Chrome (141+) should render a cross-device QR; scan it with your Android Multipaz wallet and present the credential. You'll likely see an "unverified verifier" warning — that's expected (self-signed); consent anyway. The encrypted vp_token returns through this page to the helper, which decrypts it and shows the disclosed fields.

Prerequisite: a Digital Payment Credential must already be provisioned in your Multipaz wallet (from issuer.multipaz.org or a local issuer). If you don't have one, the request returns "no matching credential."
